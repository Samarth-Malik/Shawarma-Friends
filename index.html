About us

A local kitchen doing the staples properly.

Shawarma Friends is a Middle Eastern kitchen in North Kellyville built around two things: the spit and the charcoal grill. The menu runs from quick wraps and snack packs through to mixed grills, rice mains like kabsa and mandi, and shawarma sold by the plate or by the kilo.

Sauces and dips — garlic, tahini, hummus, baba ghanoush and our own Friends sauce — are made in-house, and the bread for manoush and pide comes out of our oven through the day. Dine in, take away, or call ahead and we'll have it ready.
